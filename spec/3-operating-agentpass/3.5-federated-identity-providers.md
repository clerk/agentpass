# 3.5. Federated Identity Providers

AgentPass supports two classes of identity providers for the delegating principal:

- **Authoritative Identity Provider (AIdP):** The provider discovered from `delegating_principal_domain` via `_agentpass-authoritative.{domain}` DNS discovery, typically by Service Provider. This provider is considered the source of truth for delegation policy for that domain.
- **Federated Identity Provider (FIdP):** The fallback provider discovered from `delegating_principal_domain` via `_agentpass-federated.{domain}` DNS discovery and accepted only when explicitly trusted by Service Provider policy.

This document defines interoperability requirements for federated providers and references normative trust-mode precedence from Section `3.1`.

## Motivation

Some runtimes may provide their own identity layer (or federation) and can obtain user consent and issue AgentPass artifacts. Service Providers may choose to trust such providers in addition to (or in absence of) an authoritative enterprise provider.

## Service Provider Trust Declaration

Service Providers MAY trust multiple providers by listing accepted issuers in Service Provider metadata. Service Providers MAY further distinguish federated issuers from authoritative issuers.

## Trust-Mode Interoperability

Federated providers are used only when no Authoritative AgentPass is configured for the Delegating Principal (authoritative discovery unavailable), as defined in:

- [`3.1-authoritative-vs-federated.md`](3.1-authoritative-vs-federated.md)

When Service Provider applies federated mode, Service Provider MUST accept only issuers from its explicit trusted federated allowlist.

## Rationale

This prevents a federated provider (including a runtime platform) from bypassing an enterprise's configured delegation policy for its users.

## Required Information

To enforce the precedence rule, artifacts/tokens presented to Service Providers MUST include `delegating_principal_domain`, which is the DNS domain associated with the delegating principal.

## Notes

- Service Providers may cache authoritative discovery results according to DNS TTL.
- Service Providers should consider DNSSEC validation where available.
